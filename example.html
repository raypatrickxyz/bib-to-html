My Library:

    Computers

  • The Art of Computer Programming - Donald Knuth
    • Certainly the first comprehensive treatment of the subject, and regarded by many to be the best.
  • The Art of Unix Programming - Eric S. Raymond
    • Not your typical programming book; this one is also an analysis of the cultures surrounding early UNIX, VAX, etc.

    Cooking

  • Mastering the Art of French Cooking - Beck, S. and Bertholle, L. and Child, J.
    • A two-volume cookbook written in part by Julia Child, who popularized French cuisine in the United States following World War II.
